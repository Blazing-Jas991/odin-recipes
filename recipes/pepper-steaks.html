Pepper Steaks

Home

Description

This is a meditteranean dish made with so much love that every bite gives you a taste of home and it makes you feel like you need to be there again.

With well seasoned steaks mixed with vegetables and prepared with olive oil and delicious spices, there could not be a meal better than this.

Looking to try it at home, well, that's why we are here. Just follow the following steps to prepare your delicious Pepper steaks from start to the meal you have dreamed of eating once again.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • ¼ cup hot water
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 large green bell peppers, roughly chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    • li>

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

Step 2

Sprinkle beef sirloin strips with garlic powder. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat and sear beef strips, about 5 minutes per side. Transfer to a slow cooker.

step 3

Mix bouillon cube with hot water in a separate container until dissolved, then mix in cornstarch until dissolved.

step 4

Pour into the slow cooker with beef strips. Stir in onion, green peppers, stewed tomatoes, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.

Step 5

Cover, and cook on High for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low for 6 to 8 hours.
