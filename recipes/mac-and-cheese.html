Mac And Cheese

Home

Description

This crockpot mac and cheese is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this crockpot mac and cheese recipe:

  1. Macaroni:This classic mac and cheese recipe starts with a package of elbow macaroni.
    • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  1. Butter: A stick of butter lends richness and flavor.
    • ½ cup butter
  1. Seasonings: Simply season the macaroni and cheese with salt and pepper.
    • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  1. Cheese: You'll need shredded Cheddar cheese for this basic recipe.
    • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  1. Milk Products:The creamy cheese sauce calls for whole milk and evaporated milk.
    • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
    • 2 cups whole milk
  1. Eggs: Up the richness with two beaten eggs.
    • 2 large eggs, well beaten
  1. Canned soup: A can of condensed Cheddar cheese soup is the budget-friendly secret ingredient.
    • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed Cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
  1. Paprika: Ground paprika is optional, but recommended for fans of flavor!
    1. 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed Cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
    2. 1 pinch paprika, or as desired (Optional)

How to Make Mac and Cheese In the Slow Cooker

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this crockpot mac and cheese:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water, then drain and transfer to the slow cooker.
  2. Stir in the butter, seasonings, and about half of the Cheddar.
  3. Whisk the evaporated milk and eggs together, then stir into the pasta.
  4. Whisk the milk and the soup together, then stir into the pasta.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the pasta mixture and garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on Low for 2 ½ to 3 hours.

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

step 2

Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.

Step 3

Add butter to pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.

Step 4

Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.

Step 5

Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.

Step 6

Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.

Step 7

Cook on Low for 3 hours, checking the edges are not getting too brown after 2 ½ hours.

Step 8

Serve hot and enjoy!
